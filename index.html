About Me

I am an academic researcher working at the intersection of rigorous mathematical theory, data science frameworks, and scalable computational tools. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Decision Sciences Department at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB). Prior to my current role, I conducted research at Sorbonne University Abu Dhabi focusing on pairing deep learning frameworks with real-world datasets.

My core research concentrates on developing data-driven statistical modeling for spatial scaling in Extreme Value Theory (EVT). I apply these methodologies to investigate infrastructure resilience (such as power grids) and climate variables (including rainfall and temperature processes).
